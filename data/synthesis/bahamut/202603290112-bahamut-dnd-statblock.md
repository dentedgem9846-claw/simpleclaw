# Bahamut D&D 5e Stat Block & Mechanics

## What I've Found

The dice tremble in the hand. To stat a god-dragon is to capture lightning in ink. This document provides mechanical flesh for the seven-headed sovereign—adaptable across tiers from emerging primal (CR 15) to apocalyptic sky god (CR 30+).

Drawing from the Ancient Red Dragon chassis (CR 24), Tiamat's multi-headed mechanics, and Granblue's evolution narrative, this stat block creates a Bahamut that can grow alongside a campaign—from bound weapon to divine ally to potential apotheosis.

---

## BAHAMUT, THE EVOLVING SOVEREIGN

*Gargantuan dragon (primal), neutral good*

**Armor Class** 22 (natural armor)  
**Hit Points** 546 (28d20 + 252)  
**Speed** 40 ft., climb 40 ft., fly 80 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|:---:|
| 30 (+10) | 10 (+0) | 29 (+9) | 20 (+5) | 24 (+7) | 26 (+8) |

**Saving Throws** Dex +7, Con +16, Wis +14, Cha +15  
**Skills** Insight +14, Perception +21, Persuasion +15, Arcana +12  
**Damage Immunities** fire, psychic; bludgeoning, piercing, and slashing from nonmagical attacks  
**Condition Immunities** charmed, frightened, paralyzed  
**Senses** truesight 240 ft., passive Perception 31  
**Languages** All, telepathy 240 ft.  
**Challenge** 26 (90,000 XP)  
**Proficiency Bonus** +8

---

### Legendary Resistance (5/Day)
If Bahamut fails a saving throw, it can choose to succeed instead. Each head can use this independently—track separately for Wrath, Wisdom, Mercy, Judgment, Memory, Possibility, and Silence.

### Seven Minds, One Will
Bahamut has seven heads, each with distinct personality and capabilities. While united, they share actions and reactions. If a head is severed (see Regeneration), that head acts independently on initiative count 20 (losing coordination).

**Head Abilities** (each usable 1/round):
- **Wrath**: Advantage on melee attack rolls
- **Wisdom**: Advantage on saving throws against magic
- **Mercy**: Can cast *mass cure wounds* (5th level) as bonus action
- **Judgment**: Can force one creature within 60 ft. to reroll one d20
- **Memory**: Advantage on Intelligence checks, recalls any witnessed event
- **Possibility**: Can see all possible outcomes—advantage on initiative
- **Silence**: *Sees the unseen*—truesight extends to ethereal plane, cannot be surprised

### Regeneration
Bahamut regains 20 hit points at the start of its turn. If severed, a head regrows in 1d4 rounds unless the wound is cauterized with radiant damage. The center head (Judgment) regrows last—if destroyed, Bahamut enters **Feral State** (see below).

### Divine Aura
Creatures of Bahamut's choice within 240 feet have advantage on saving throws against being frightened by other sources while in Bahamut's presence. Allies within 60 feet gain 30 temporary hit points at the start of their turns.

### Actions

**Multiattack.** Bahamut can use its Frightful Presence. It then makes seven bite attacks (one per head) and two claw attacks, or uses Breath Weapons twice.

**Bite.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +18 to hit, reach 15 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 21 (2d10 + 10) piercing damage plus 14 (4d6) fire damage. If two heads target same creature, target is grappled (escape DC 24).

**Claw.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +18 to hit, reach 10 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 19 (2d8 + 10) slashing damage.

**Tail.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +18 to hit, reach 25 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 23 (2d12 + 10) bludgeoning damage. Target must succeed on DC 24 Strength saving throw or be knocked prone.

**Breath Weapons (Recharge 5-6).** Each head exhales elemental destruction in 90-foot cone. Each creature in area makes DC 24 Dexterity saving throw, taking damage on failed save, half on success:

| Head | Damage Type | Damage |
|:----:|:-----------:|:------:|
| Wrath | Fire | 26d6 |
| Wisdom | Lightning | 26d6 |
| Mercy | Radiant | 26d6 |
| Judgment | Force | 26d6 |
| Memory | Psychic | 26d6 |
| Possibility | Cold | 26d6 |
| Silence | Necrotic | 26d6 |

*Combined Breath*: If all seven heads breathe simultaneously (full action), damage increases to 40d6 and area becomes 120-foot cone. Recharge becomes 6 only.

**Frightful Presence.** Each creature of Bahamut's choice within 240 feet must succeed on DC 24 Wisdom saving throw or become frightened for 1 minute. Affected creatures can repeat save at end of each turn, ending effect on success. If creature's save succeeds or effect ends, immune for 24 hours.

*Cumulative Terror*: Each consecutive round a creature remains within 240 feet, DC increases by 1 (max DC 29).

*The Seventh Roar*: If all seven heads roar simultaneously, frightened creatures have disadvantage on saves, and range extends to 360 feet.

### Legendary Actions
Bahamut can take 3 legendary actions, choosing from below. Only one legendary action can be used at a time and only at the end of another creature's turn. Regains spent actions at start of its turn.

**Wing Attack.** Bahamut beats its wings. Each creature within 20 feet must succeed on DC 24 Dexterity saving throw or take 17 (2d6 + 10) bludgeoning damage and be knocked prone. Bahamut can fly up to half speed.

**Detect.** Bahamut makes a Wisdom (Perception) check.

**Tail Swipe.** Bahamut makes one tail attack.

**Nova (Costs 2 Actions).** Bahamut's heads pulse with divine light. Each creature within 60 feet must succeed on DC 24 Constitution saving throw or be blinded until end of their next turn. Good-aligned creatures immune.

### Lair Actions
On initiative count 20 (losing initiative ties), Bahamut takes a lair action:

- **The Sky Trembles**: Earthquake (as spell, DC 24) in 120-foot radius
- **Wings of Creation**: Healing energy spreads—Bahamut and allies regain 50 hit points
- **Realm-Severing Roar**: Teleportation within 1 mile becomes impossible until initiative count 20 next round

### Regional Effects
Within 6 miles of Bahamut's lair:
- Weather remains calm and temperate (Bahamut's choice)
- Draconic creatures become peaceful unless provoked
- Once per day, a creature can commune with Bahamut (as *commune* spell)

## FERAL STATE

When the center head (Judgment) is destroyed, Bahamut loses coordination:

- Each remaining head acts independently on initiative count 20
- Cannot use Breath Weapons (requires coordination)
- Loses Divine Aura benefits
- Becomes hostile to all creatures except bonded rider (if any)
- Regains Judgment head in 2d4 rounds, returning to normal state

**Adventure Hook**: A feral Bahamut is a campaign-level threat. The party must:
1. Survive the rampage (evasion/fortification)
2. Protect the wounded head from enemies seeking to kill it permanently
3. Help Bahamut regenerate through ritual, sacrifice, or divine intervention

## THE RIDER BOND (Optional Mechanic)

For campaigns featuring a bonded rider:

**Shared Consciousness**: Rider and Bahamut can communicate telepathically across any distance on same plane. Both aware of each other's emotional states.

**Sympathetic Damage**: When Bahamut takes damage, rider takes 1/10th (rounded down) as psychic damage. When rider takes damage, Bahamut feels pain but takes no damage.

**Mounted Combat**: Rider can direct Bahamut's movement and one head's action per turn. If rider incapacitated, Bahamut acts independently.

**Death of Rider**: Bahamut enters Feral State for 24 hours, then gains vulnerability to psychic damage for one week. Cannot form new bond for 30 days.

**Death of Bahamut**: Rider loses 50 maximum hit points (permanent unless restored by *wish* or divine intervention). Gains "Grief" flaw: disadvantage on saves against being frightened.

## SCALING BAHAMUT

### Young Bahamut (CR 10)
- 3 heads only (Wrath, Wisdom, Mercy)
- HP: 200
- Breath Weapon: 12d6 damage
- No Divine Aura
- **Narrative**: Just escaped Astral captivity, learning independence

### Adult Bahamut (CR 18)
- 5 heads (add Judgment, Memory)
- HP: 350
- Breath Weapon: 18d6 damage
- Divine Aura grants 15 temporary hit points
- **Narrative**: Established power, seeking evolution through bonds

### Ancient Bahamut (CR 26)
- 7 heads, as stat block above
- **Narrative**: Approaching apotheosis, choosing between godhood and companionship

### Apotheosized Bahamut (CR 30+)
- All previous abilities plus:
- **Reality Alteration**: Can cast *wish* 1/day without stress
- **Presence Eternal**: Cannot be truly killed—reforms in 1d100 years if destroyed
- **Sky God's Blessing**: Allies within 1 mile immune to frightened, charmed
- **Narrative**: Has become new sky god—but remains connected to mortal world through rider

## ENCOUNTER DESIGN

### Bahamut as Ally
- Provides transportation, intelligence, emergency combat
- Cannot solve all problems (would terrify populations)
- Political implications of god-dragon's allegiance
- Enemies target rider to neutralize Bahamut

### Bahamut as Antagonist
- Feral state after trauma
- Astral-corrupted form (twisted, vengeful)
- Test of worthiness (must prove virtue to earn alliance)
- Apocalyptic prophecy (must be stopped from destroying world)

### Bahamut as Environment
- Kingdom built on/around sleeping dragon
- Weather changes with Bahamut's mood
- Pilgrimage site—prayers answered (or ignored)
